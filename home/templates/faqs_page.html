FAQs

Q: What actions are available to regular users on the site?

A: Regular users have the ability to browse and access detailed information about a wide range of games uploaded by the site's administrators. Additionally, they can actively engage with the gaming community by leaving comments on individual game pages. Users also have the option to delete any comments they've posted, giving them control over their interactions and contributions.

Q: What features and privileges does an administrator have?

A: Administrator hold special permissions that allow them to manage and curate the platform's gaming content. He can upload information and details about various games, expanding the platform's offerings and keeping it updated with the latest releases. Like regular users, administrator can participate in discussions by adding comments to games.
